Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

What industries do you primarily serve?
We serve a wide range of industries including automotive, aerospace, heavy engineering, tractor & farm equipment manufacturing, and general precision engineering sectors. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific requirements of each industry.
How quickly can you respond to technical support requests?
Our standard response time for technical support is within 4 hours during business days. For emergency support, we offer 24/7 service for our premium clients with a guaranteed response within 2 hours.
Do you provide custom engineering solutions?
Yes, we specialize in custom engineering solutions. Our team works closely with clients to understand their specific requirements and develop tailored tooling solutions that optimize performance and efficiency for their unique applications.
What is your typical delivery timeline?
Standard products are typically shipped within 3-5 business days. For custom solutions, delivery timelines vary based on complexity but generally range from 2-6 weeks. We provide detailed project timelines during the quotation process.
Do you offer training for your products?
Yes, we provide comprehensive training programs for all our products. This includes on-site training, virtual sessions, and detailed documentation. We also offer advanced technical workshops for engineering teams.
